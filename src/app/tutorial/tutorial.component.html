What is Draftmon ?

In Draftmon, create or join rooms where you can draft competitive pokémon sets.

The objective is to build the strongest team by strategically selecting pokémons one after another.

  • Each player opens their first pack, chooses a pokémon, then passes the rest to the next player.
  • Players then receive pokémons from the preceding player, select another one, and pass the remainder along again.
  • This process repeats for each pokémon in the pack and for each pack... The number of pokémons in each pack, as well as the number of packs are adjustable. The direction of rotation swaps with each new pack!

The key to success here is making the right decisions. Pokémons have different types, moves, statistics, items, and abilities, and it's up to you to choose the one that will join your team. Will you prioritize raw power over synergies? Will you compensate for your weaknesses or go all in on your strengths?

You now have a pool of available pokémons to compute your final six. Once your team is ready, export it in a format compatible with Pokémon Showdown and compete with the players you drafted with.

How can I import my team in Pokémon Showdown?

You now leave Draftmon with your final team copied to the clipboard and heads toward the battling grounds of Pokémon Showdown.
  • Connect to Pokémon Showdown (if it's the first time, click [choose name], [⚙️] and [register]).
  • Click [TeamBuilder].
  • Click [New Team].
  • Click [Import from text or URL].
  • Paste your team.
  • Click [Save].
  • Set [Format]: Custom Game (In S/V singles, halfway down the list).
  • Put the first pokémon you want to send out at the top of your team using [move].

Everything is now good to start battling!

How can I challenge my favorite nemesis on Pokémon Showdown?

You or your opponent has to invite the other to a direct challenge!
  • Go straight to the Home page of Pokémon Showdown.
  • Click [Find a user].
  • Type the sweet name of your opponent [open].
  • Click [chat].
  • A new chat window appears at the top of the page.
  • Type (click to copy) : /challenge [Gen 9] Custom Game @@@ !Team Preview .
  • The challenge request has been sent!
  • Once the other player clicks [accept], the battle is on!!!
