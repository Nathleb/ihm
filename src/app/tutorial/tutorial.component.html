What is Draftmon ?

In Draftmon you create or join rooms where you can draft competitive pokemon sets.

The objective is to build the strongest team by strategically selecting pokemons.

  • Each player opens their first pack, chooses a pokemon, then passes the rest to the next player.
  • Players then receive pokemons from the preceding player, choose another one, and pass the remainder along again.
  • This process repeats for each pokemon in the pack and for each pack... The direction of rotation swaps with each new pack!

The key to success here is making the right decisions. Pokémon have different types, moves, statistics, items, and abilities, and it's up to you to choose the one that will join your team. Will you prioritize raw power over synergies? Will you compensate for your weaknesses or go all in on your strengths?

You now have a pool of available pokemons to compute your final six. Once your team ready, you can export it in a format compatible with Pokemon Showdown and compete with the players you drafted with.

How can I import my team in Pokemon Showdown?

You now leave Draftmon with your final team copied to the clipboard and heads toward the battling grounds of Pokémon Showdown.
  • Connect to Pokemon Showdown (if it's the first time, click [choose name], [⚙️] and [register]).
  • Click [TeamBuilder].
  • Click [New Team].
  • Click [Import from text or URL].
  • Paste your team.
  • Click [Save].
  • Set [Format]: Anything Goes (In S/V singles, halfway down the list).
  • Put the first pokemon you want to send out at the top of your team using [move].

Everything is now good to start battling!

How can I challenge my favourite nemesis on Pokemon Showdown?

You or your opponent has to invite the other to a direct challenge!
  • Go straigth to the Home page of Pokemon Showdown.
  • Click [Find a user].
  • Type the sweet name of your opponent [open].
  • Click [chat].
  • A new chat window appears at the top of the page.
  • Type (click to copy) : /challenge [Gen 9] Custom Game @@@ !Team Preview .
  • The challenge request has been sent!
  • Once the other player clicks [accept] the battle is on!!!
