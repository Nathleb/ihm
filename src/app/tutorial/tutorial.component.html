What is Draftmon ?

Draftmon allows you to create or join rooms where you can draft competitive pokemon sets.

The objective is to build the strongest team by strategically selecting pokemons.

  • Each player opens their first pack, chooses a pokemon, then passes the rest to the next player.
  • Players then receive pokemon from the preceding player, choose another one, and pass the remainder along again.
  • This process repeats for each pokemon in the pack and for each pack... The direction of rotation swaps with each new pack!

At the end of the draft, you can export your team in a format compatible with Pokemon Showdown and compete with the players you drafted with.
